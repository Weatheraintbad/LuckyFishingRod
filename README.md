### 幸运钓竿(Lucky Fishing Rod) luckyfishingrod:lucky_fishing_rod 在创造模式下位于原版工具栏，可使用萤石、红石和原版钓鱼竿合成。
Lucky Fishing Rod is located in the original toolbar in Creative Mode and can be crafted using Glowstone dust, Redstone, and the original fishing rod.

### 支持任何模组，只要是服务端/客户端成功加载的物品都有可能会被钓上来。
Support any mod, as long as the item is successfully loaded by the server/client, it may be fished.

### 在原版我的世界1.20.1中使用幸运钓竿钓上鱼（生鳕鱼、生蛙鱼、热带鱼、河豚）的概率约为0.96%。
The probability of catching fish (raw cod, raw frogfish, tropical fish, pufferfish) with Lucky Fishing Rod in original Minecraft 1.20.1 is about 0.96%.
